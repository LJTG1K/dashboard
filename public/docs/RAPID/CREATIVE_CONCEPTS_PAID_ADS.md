# RAPID Paid Ad Concepts — Cold Audience Introduction

**Format:** Short-form video (15-30 seconds for ads)  
**Platform:** TikTok Ads, Instagram Reels Ads, YouTube Shorts Ads  
**Target:** Cold audience unfamiliar with agents/Chinese shopping. People who want cheap fashion.  
**Goal:** Convert to RAPID user → naturally use Sugargoo logistics  
**Tone:** Simple, exciting, accessible. Remove jargon. Show the problem → solution → result.  
**Style:** Hook hard (first 2s), explain simply (next 8s), show proof/CTA (final 5-8s)

---

## Core Strategy for Cold Audiences

**They don't know:**
- What an agent is
- That Chinese platforms exist
- How shipping from China works
- That RAPID even exists

**They DO know:**
- Fashion costs a lot
- They want deals
- Designer stuff is expensive
- TikTok/influencers show them cool finds

**Your job:** Show them the shortcut they didn't know existed.

**Formula for each ad:**
1. **Hook (0-2s):** Show the problem or curiosity-gap ("Wait, how is this so cheap?")
2. **Explain (2-10s):** Show what RAPID is in 15 seconds or less (no jargon)
3. **Proof (10-15s):** Show results, testimonials, or product quality
4. **CTA (15-30s):** Direct link, simple ask

## 1. "How Is This Only $15? (The Secret TikTok Doesn't Tell You)"

**Hook:** Show an expensive item (Essentials hoodie, Nike tee, designer streetwear) then cut to same item for 1/10 the price. Confused face.

**Concept:** This format stops the scroll. "Wait, where do I get it THAT cheap?" Then reveal: RAPID. Simple.

**Outline:**
- 0-1s: Show Essentials hoodie on retail site ($250)
- 1-2s: Show same hoodie on RAPID ($25) — shocked reaction
- 2-5s: "RAPID connects you to sellers in China who make these for cheap"
- 5-8s: Quick explanation: "You pick items, RAPID handles the shipping for you"
- 8-12s: Show 3-4 more product examples with price comparisons
- 12-15s: "It's safe, tracked, and arrives in 2-3 weeks"
- 15-30s: CTA: "Link in bio. New to RAPID? Start with $5 free credit"

**Why it works:** Price difference is the hook. People click because they want to know the secret.

## Key Principles for Cold Audience Ads

✅ **First 2 seconds are EVERYTHING** — Hook hard or they scroll  
✅ **Remove all jargon** — No "agent," "Sugargoo," "logistics" without explaining  
✅ **Show the problem first** — "Fashion is expensive" before "RAPID is the solution"  
✅ **Proof matters** — Testimonials, unboxings, side-by-sides  
✅ **Address objections upfront** — "Is it safe? Is it legal? Will it arrive?"  
✅ **Specific comparisons** — "Urban Outfitters $150 vs RAPID $18" (concrete, not vague)  
✅ **Timeline expectations** — "2-3 weeks" not "fast shipping"  
✅ **Simple CTA** — "Link in bio" or "Click to shop"  
✅ **No gatekeeping** — This should feel ACCESSIBLE not exclusive  
✅ **End with aspirational feeling** — People should feel smart for learning this, not dumb for not knowing  

---

## What NOT To Do (For Cold Audiences)

❌ Don't assume they know what an agent is  
❌ Don't use Reddit/reps terminology (QC, haul, etc.)  
❌ Don't make it sound sketchy (it's not, but language matters)  
❌ Don't oversell (set expectations: 2-3 weeks, not 1 week)  
❌ Don't ignore objections (address safety, legality, quality upfront)  
❌ Don't use only text (people watch videos, need visual proof)  
❌ Don't compare to other platforms without being honest

## Ad Performance Tips

**Best performing hooks for cold audiences:**
- Price comparisons (retail $X vs RAPID $Y)
- "What I didn't know" / transformation story
- "Is this legal/safe?" (objection-driven)
- Unboxing/proof (real products)
- "Here's how X much money I saved"

**Best audience segments to target:**
- Interests: Streetwear, fashion, budget shopping
- Age: 18-35 (TikTok native)
- Lookalike audiences from Reddit fashion communities
- People who've bought from Taobao before
- Fashion enthusiasts on a budget

**Test sequence:**
1. Start with concepts 1, 4, 7 (price hook, relatable, proof)
2. Measure: CTR, CPC, conversion rate
3. Double down on winners
4. Rotate in new creative every 2 weeks
5. Use testimonials/UGC once you have users
