# RAPID Video Ad Concepts (@gilly1k Style)

**Format:** Short-form video (15-60 seconds)  
**Platform:** TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts  
**Target:** r/FashionReps community, budget-conscious fashion enthusiasts  
**Tone:** Fast-paced, humor-driven, trendy, relatable

---

## 1. "POV: You Found The Haul Plug"

**Hook:** "POV: Your agent told you they don't do bundles... 😭"

**Concept:** Quick cuts of scrolling through RAPID's massive catalog, showing variety and abundance. Cut to reaction of realizing you can get 10+ items in one order.

**Vibe:** Overwhelmed-in-a-good-way energy. Fast transitions. Background: trending audio about "finding the one."

**Outline:**
- 0-3s: Sad face, scrolling through limited options
- 3-8s: Jump cut to RAPID catalog reveal (trending song peaks)
- 8-12s: Quick product cuts (hoodies, tees, accessories, shoes)
- 12-15s: "Wait, how much?" → Price reveal shock face
- 15s: CTA text overlay

## Production Notes (@gilly1k Style Checklist)

✅ **Pacing:** Fast cuts every 1-3 seconds  
✅ **Music:** Trending audio + beat drops for hype moments  
✅ **Transitions:** Quick zooms, match cuts, text overlays (not overdone)  
✅ **Reactions:** Natural, genuine, sometimes exaggerated for humor  
✅ **Text:** Minimal, punchy, emojis sparingly  
✅ **Captions:** Always (vertical video + sound-off viewers)  
✅ **Lighting:** Phone-quality acceptable, natural daylight preferred  
✅ **CTA:** Last frame always directs to RAPID link or "Link in bio"
