## Status Legend

- **🟡 Planned** — On target list, not contacted yet
- **🟠 Contacted** — Initial message sent, waiting response
- **🟢 Confirmed** — Meeting scheduled
- **✅ Met** — Meeting completed
- **📝 Negotiating** — Contract discussion in progress
- **✔️ Signed** — Contract signed
- **🚀 Live** — Products added to RAPID
- **❌ Lost** — Declined partnership

---

## Interest Level Scoring

| Score | Meaning | Action |
|-------|---------|--------|
| ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Extremely interested, ready to sign | Priority follow-up, close deal fast |
| ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Very interested, likes proposal | Send contract within 24h |
| ⭐⭐⭐ | Interested, needs to think/discuss | Follow up in 1 week |
| ⭐⭐ | Somewhat interested, hesitant | Multiple follow-ups, address concerns |
| ⭐ | Not interested, unlikely to convert | Note reason, don't pursue |

---

## Deal Type Options

- **EX** — Exclusive products (50/50 profit share)
- **REV** — Revenue share (10-15% commission)
- **AD** — Advertising partnership ($500-2000/mo)
- **LIST** — Standard listing (commission only)
- **MULTI** — Multiple deal types
