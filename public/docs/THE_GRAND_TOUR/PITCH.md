## Key Principles for Delivery

1. **Speak their language (literally)** — Use translator, have bilingual slides
2. **Show, don't tell** — Live demo of RAPID platform on phone/laptop
3. **Social proof** — Screenshots of satisfied customers, testimonials
4. **Keep it short** — 10 minutes max. Rest is conversation.
5. **Be honest** — Don't oversell. Explain commissions/fees clearly.
6. **Give options** — Exclusive deal OR advertising. Let them choose.
7. **Start small** — "Try 10 products first, no commitment."

---

## Translator Notes

When working with translator:
- **Pre-translate** these slides and practice pronunciation
- **Key phrases to nail:**
  - "RAPID makes it easy"
  - "You focus on products, we handle the rest"
  - "No shipping headaches"
  - "Global customers want your products"
  - "Let's start small and grow together"
- **Cultural notes:** 
  - Don't be too pushy (respect decision-making process)
  - Emphasize long-term partnership, not quick cash grab
  - Offer tea/coffee, be patient, build relationship
